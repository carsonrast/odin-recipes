Braised Shank

Description

This savory dish makes full use out of time and heat to create a melt-in-your-mouth plate of delicious lamb.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 4 lamb shanks
  • 1 dash salt
  • 1 dash freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 325.
  3. In a large dutch oven, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Add the onion, celery, and carrot. Cook, stirring until onion is softened.
  4. Add the garlic and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes more. Remove to a large baking pan. Add 2 more tablespoons of live oil to the pan. Sprinkle the lamb shanks with salt and pepper; saute over medium heat for about 8 minutes, turning to sear all sides.
  5. Add the lamb to the Dutch oven or pan with the chopped vegetables. In the same skillet, deglaze with the red wine. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the tomato paste, chicken broth, beef brother, and vinegar. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Pour over the lamb shanks and add the fresh thyme and bay leaf.
  7. Cover the pan tightly and bake for 1 1/2 hours. Remove the foil or lid and continue baking for 2 to 2 1/2 hours longer, turning the shanks occasionally. The lamb should be very tender.
  8. Serve with mashed potatoes and roasted vegetables.
