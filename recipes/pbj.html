Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich


This homerun of a sandwich is every kid's favorite. A must-have in the sandwich afficionado's repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of jelly of choice
  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Spread onto one side of one piece of bread the peanut butter.
  3. Spread onto one side of the other piece of bread the jelly.
  4. Combine the slices, jelly and peanut butter side together, and enjoy the best sandwich known to man.
